The command string is an 11 byte string of the format

00 XX 06 ZZ 00 0b 00 06 <command> 00 ff 

Where XX is a sequence number that is not used by the system and ZZ is the
zone number:

Zone 1 = 21
Zone 2 = 22
Zone 3 = 23
Zone 4 = 24
Zone 5 = 25
Zone 6 = 26

Treble down   83

Mute/Unmute   0b  (toggle)
